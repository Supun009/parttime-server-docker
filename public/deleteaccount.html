How to Delete Your Account

If you want to delete your account, you can easily do so from within the app. Just follow these steps:

  1. Open the app and log in to your account.
  2. Go to the menu by tapping on the menu icon.
  3. Select Profile from the menu.
  4. Scroll down and tap on Delete Account.
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to permanently delete your account.

If you encounter any issues or are unable to delete your account, please send us an email with your request, and we will assist you as soon as possible.
